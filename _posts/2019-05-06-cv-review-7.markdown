# Affine Transformations

## Definition 

We call any 3 by 3 matrix with last row $$\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$$ an affine transformation:  $$ \begin{bmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$
